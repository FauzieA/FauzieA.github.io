OIC Plan of Action for the Advancement of Women (OPAAW)

The Organisation of Islamic Cooperation's (OIC) Plan of Action for the Advandement of Women (OPAAW), adopted in 2008, is aimed at improving the status of women in its 57 member countries by advancing their rights and opportunities. This all-encompassing plan addresses key areas of women's life outcomes, such as education, healthcare, economic participation, and legal protection.
